JF - ABOUT

I am not an artist.

I am not an artist.

An artist creates the unique. The original. I simply borrow, steal, and take from others. I do not build, but rather assemble. I do not innovate, I just adapt.

My work exists solely in the digital world. It is at once incredibly fragile and at the same time, eternal. Everything I create is an illusion; a dishonest representation of something from the physical world. It is just a carefully compiled collection of 1’s and 0’s. It is not real. I hope to explore the limits of digital media through the evolution of technology. I am not an artist.

I have no workflow. No set of parameters or guidelines that I follow. I select material that I believe to be aesthetically interesting. I like to think I have a good eye for what looks good and what doesn’t. When it comes to making decisions in the course of my work, I simply go with my gut. It hasn’t failed me just yet.

I do not have a message to send to the masses. No injustice I’d like to bring to light. I’m just a thief who happens to love digital exploration. The way I see it, the writing is on the wall; the digital realm will consume every aspect of our lives. Soon. I just hope to make it as visually interesting as I can.
